Facilities

FSK konsentreer hoofsaaklik op die luggeweer en lug pistool skyfskiet sport op skole vlak onder die riglyne van SANSSU
(Suid Afrikaanse Nasionale Skole Skutters Unie)

Leerders van hoofsaaklik H/S Florida is aktief by die FSK. Daar is ook leerders van ander skole betrokke by FSK, aangesien hulle skole nie skyfskiet aanbied nie.

Shooting

60 x 10m AIRRIFLE LANES (3 positional capable) with movable multi-diagram targets with LED lighting
(Currently 12 lanes used for Target runners system on 10m)

Shooting position tables

Gun workshop on premises

Tuck Shop available

Events

All shooting position tables can be removed

Full ablution facilites for at least 500 people

Under cover veranda area

Under cover braai facilities

Ample parking facilities available
